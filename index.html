PancakeSwap

PancakeSwap is a decentralized exchange (DEX) built on BNB Chain that utilizes an automated market making (AMM) system. It is a fork of SushiSwap, with an almost identical codebase, but it has the advantage of cheaper and faster transactions due to being built on BSC. Additionally, it offers features such as yield farming across other protocols, lotteries, and initial farm offerings (IFO).

Binance web3 wallet

Binance Web3 Wallet is a self-custody crypto wallet within the Binance app, designed to empower users in the realm of decentralized finance (DeFi). Serving as a digital gateway to blockchain-based applications (dApps), it offers users a secure and streamlined method to manage their cryptocurrencies, execute token swaps across multiple chains, earn yields, and interact with a variety of blockchain platforms.

Trust wallet

Trust Wallet is a popular cryptocurrency hot wallet that allows users to retain control over their funds. A central feature of Trust Wallet is its in-built DApp browser, which offers a selection of vetted decentralized applications. Trust Wallet also offers staking and token swaps straight in the app.

Safepal

SafePal is dedicated to providing a secure and user-friendly crypto management platform to help the masses secure and grow their crypto assets safely and conveniently. It is the first hardware wallet invested and backed by Binance, and also the first decentralized cryptocurrency wallet to implement the Binance trading experience.

Gate web3

Gate Web3 Wallet is a powerful multi-chain aggregation, non-custodial digital asset management tool designed to empower users to independently control and manage assets across different chains easily.

Coin98

Coin98 is a DeFi gateway that enables TraFi users to access various DeFi services on multiple blockchains, such as decentralized exchanges, lending protocols, on-chain governance, blockchain-based games, and cross-chain transfers on over 20 networks like Ethereum, Solana, Avalanche, and more.

Token Pocket

TokenPocket is a multi-chain decentralized wallet, it provides users with mobile wallet, extension wallet and hardware wallet, supporting public chains including Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB Smart Chain, TRON, Aptos, Polygon, Solana, Polkadot, EOS and all EVM compatible chains. Serving over 20 millions users from more than 200 countries and regions. It is a world-wide leading crypto wallet that trusted by global users.

AScoin

AScoin Wallet is a Web3.0 multi-chain crypto aggregation service platform, dedicated to becoming the traffic portal for millions of users transitioning from Web2.0 to Web3.0. It is integrated with mainstream chains and thousands of assets, providing various functions and modules in one app to satisfy diversified needs, such as Self-custodial Asset Storage, Centralized Exchange Trading, Flash Swap, DApps Interaction, NFT, Chat and more.

Allsto

AllsTo is a decentralized payment service that simplifies the process of collecting stablecoin payments. Users can create a payment link with any chain and stablecoin of their choice and share it to collect payments. The platform allows users to use stablecoins for various types of personal payments and enterprise payment management.

QNA3

QnA3 is an AI-powered Web3 Knowledge Sharing Platform. That aims to be one of the most accurate crypto products with knowledge in the field of cryptocurrencies.
